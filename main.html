About Us

SignSpeak At Hand Sign Interpreter, we are dedicated to breaking communication barriers and creating a more inclusive world. Our mission is to empower the deaf and hard-of-hearing community by seamlessly connecting sign language users and non-signers through innovative technology. Our platform translates hand signs into text or speech in real time, enabling effortless communication in everyday interactions, workplaces, educational settings, and beyond. Whether you're bridging language gaps or learning sign language, Hand Sign Interpreter is here to support you..
